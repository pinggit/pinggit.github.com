快餐常客必会的点餐英语

Table of Contents

经常去麦当劳、肯德基的朋友们一定要看看了。

May I help you? / What can I get for you today?
Yes. number one combo. And one Big Mac with medium fries。

For here or to go?
For here,

With cheese?
No. Thanks。

Would you like something to drink? / What kinds of drinks?
Yes, one small Sprite please.

Cream or sugar?
Both.

Would you like fries to go with that?
Yes, a big one。

Is that all? / Anything else?
That's it. / That's all。

Five dollars and fifty five cents。
Here is six dollars。

Here is forty five cents, have a great day!
Thanks. / You too!

  1. 套餐 (combo) 通常看着图片就知道几号是什么套餐，点套餐时只要说 Combo number one，或是 number two 就好了。有时店员会问Do you want to go large? 如果你需要，他会给你大的薯条 和可乐。

  2. 汉堡 (hamburger) 老美说汉堡通常会说 burger， 如 cheese burger。在美国单点汉堡店员会问你 goes with cheese? 所以也可以在点的时候就说 without cheese，他就不会再问你了。有些比 较好的餐厅还会问你肉要几分熟，有意思吧。

  3. 薯条 (fries)

  4. 饮料 (drinks) 在快餐店的饮料就那么几种：

    1. 可乐 coke

    2. 减肥可乐 diet coke (diet)

    3. 雪碧 Sprite

    4. 橘子汽水 Hi-C (or orange soda)

    5. 冰茶 (iced tea)

soft drink 大多数是可以 refill 的
